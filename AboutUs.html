About Us

9REVISAR© is a website made by a talented group of Grade 9 students of SY.2023-2024 that aims to help future Grade 9 students to have a fun and easier way of learning.

We offer a variety of presentations, reviewers, and videos for different kinds of school subjects that can help ease the task of studying, taking notes, and reviewing for upcomiong quizzes or exams.

We, who personally has experienced what it's like being confused with all the different subject lectures, would like you to experience a more organized way of learning. we aim to make studying more fun and easier by making a website where EVERYTHING you need regarding academics is there, including lectures, a to-do list, and many more to be added! Studying can be easy as one button click away.

So we welcome you with open arms! To help you open doors, opportunities, and study with ease. Welcome to 9REVISAR©!

Our Mission

Our mission at 9REVISAR© is to help Grade 9 students with organization of notes, assignments, and reviewers. Our mission is to help lessen the confusion and stress of studying. We aspire to deliver a reliable, user-friendly website that helps with learning to not only save time but to also confidently walk into your classroom well informed for an upcoming quiz!

Our Vision

We'd like to make a website where all your ascademic needs can be solved in one website. Our Vision is to be a Website where future Grade 9 students can study easier, to inspire them to learn due to accessibility of lectures. We envision a platform that not only promotes academic success but also provides future Grade 9 students with knowledge, and a desire for continuous improvement.
